Spaghetti Aglio e Olio (Spaghetti with Garlic & Olive Oil)

Ingredients:

  • 400g (14 oz) spaghetti
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
  • ½ cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan or Pecorino cheese (optional)

Instructions:-

1️⃣ Boil the pasta

  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  • Add spaghetti and cook until al dente according to package instructions.
  • Reserve 1 cup of pasta cooking water, then drain.

2️⃣ Prepare the sauce

  • While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  • Add sliced garlic and cook gently, stirring often, until it just begins to turn golden (1–2 minutes).
  • Add red pepper flakes if using; cook 30 seconds more.

3️⃣ Combine pasta and sauce

  • Add drained spaghetti directly to the skillet with garlic and oil.
  • Pour in about ½ cup reserved pasta water and toss to coat.

4️⃣ Finish with herbs

  • Stir in chopped parsley.
  • Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  • If pasta seems dry, add more reserved water a little at a time until glossy.

5️⃣ Serve

  • Divide onto plates and top with grated cheese if desired.
  • Serve immediately.
